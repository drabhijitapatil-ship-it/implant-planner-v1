<original_problem_statement>
The user wants to build a mobile app using Expo for the Department of Prosthodontics to plan and manage dental implant procedures.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**

1.  **User Roles & Access Control:**
    *   **Postgraduate (PG) Student:** Can add new patient procedures, fill out the checklist, and view a consolidated dashboard of scheduled/open slots.
    *   **Instructor (Supervisor):** Can view/edit/approve procedures they are assigned to.
    *   **Implant Incharge:** Can view/edit/approve all procedures.
    *   **Administrator:** Has full access to the application, including managing users.
    *   **Nurse:** Read-only access to view the dashboard of approved, scheduled patients.

2.  **Core Workflow (Phase-Based):**
    *   **Phase 1 (Pre-Surgical):** A PG student fills out patient details and the Pre-Surgical Protocol section of the checklist.
    *   **Phase 1 Approval:** The submission is sent to the assigned Instructor and an Implant Incharge. BOTH must approve it.
    *   **Phase 2 (Surgical):** Once Phase 1 is approved, the Surgical Protocol part of the checklist becomes active.
    *   **Phase 2 Submission:** After the procedure, the PG student fills out the Surgical Protocol section.
    *   **Phase 2 Approval:** The Phase 2 submission is sent to the Instructor and Implant Incharge for final approval.

3.  **Features:**
    *   **Data Entry Form:** Fields for PG name, Patient name, Instructor, Implant Incharge, Registration number, implant site, Receipt number, Amount paid, and procedure date/time.
    *   **Digital Checklist:** A form that is split into Pre-Surgical Protocol and Surgical Protocol.
    *   **Dashboard:** A consolidated schedule/calendar view showing filled and open slots.
    *   **Scheduling:** Time slots are from 9:30 am to 2 PM (Mon-Fri) and 10 am on Saturday.
    *   **In-App Notifications:** Alerts for Instructors and Implant Incharge on new submissions and status changes.
    *   **PDF Export:** Ability to export a PDF report of a procedure case after it is fully completed (Phase 2 approved).

4.  **Pre-populated Users:**
    *   A specific list of faculty and students needs to be pre-loaded into the system with their designated roles.

</original_problem_statement>
