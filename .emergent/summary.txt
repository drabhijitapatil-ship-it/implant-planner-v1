    *   **Postgraduate (PG) Student:** Can add new patient procedures, fill out checklists, and view their own procedures.
    *   **Supervisor:** Can view/edit/approve procedures they are assigned to.

    *   A PG student creates a new procedure, filling patient details and submitting for dual approval from a Supervisor and Implant Incharge.
    *   Once Phase 1 is approved, the Surgical Protocol (Phase 2) becomes active.
    *   The student completes the Phase 2 checklist for final approval.
    *   A fully approved procedure is marked as Stage 1 Implant Placement Done Successfully.

3.  **Features:**
    *   **Data Entry Form:** Comprehensive form with a calendar, time slots, and styled dropdowns.
    *   **Scheduling Restrictions:** No appointments less than 24 hours in advance, limited Saturday slots, and no Sundays.
    *   **Profile Photos:** Users can upload a profile photograph.
    *   **Push Notifications:** Supervisors receive real-time alerts when a new procedure requires their approval. Students are notified of status changes.
    *   **User Management:** Admins/Incharges can manage users.
    *   **PDF Export:** A button to export completed procedures as a PDF.
